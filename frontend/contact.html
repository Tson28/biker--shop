Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I sell my bike on BikerHUB?

Selling your bike on BikerHUB is easy! Simply create an account, click "Sell Your Bike," fill out the form with details and photos, and set your price. We'll review your listing and publish it within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

How do you ensure the quality of bikes sold?

We have a comprehensive verification process that includes photo verification, seller background checks, and detailed condition reports. We also offer buyer protection and escrow services for high-value transactions.

What if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, contact our support team within 30 days and we'll work to resolve the issue or process a refund.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 150 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can calculate shipping costs during checkout before completing your purchase.

How can I become a verified seller?

To become a verified seller, complete your profile, provide government-issued ID verification, and maintain a positive rating. Verified sellers get priority listing placement and reduced fees.
